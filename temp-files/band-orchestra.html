Strait Music’s Band & Orchestra

Strait Music’s Band and Orchestra Department meets the needs of every player, from the absolute beginner to the seasoned professional. We offer an affordable rental program with purchase option for the beginning student and Austin’s best selection of intermediate and professional instruments for more experienced players. We stock a full range of accessories including tuners, metronomes, mouthpieces, ligatures, rosin, strings, cleaning supplies, mutes, and cases for all instruments.

Why Strait?

  • We work closely with area band directors to provide you with the latest approved instruments and accessories.
  • Our rent-to-own plans include FREE repair and maintenance!
  • Full-service repair on-site
  • New and used woodwinds, brass, strings, and accessories

Nathan Linn Band & Orchestra Department Manager

As a native Texan, Nathan started his customer service career at the age of 14 working in his grandmother’s antique stores in Huntsville. He is a graduate of Sam Houston State University where he studied Voice, Music Education, and Musical Theatre. Nathan has been singing for 15 years, performing in numerous operas, choirs, rock bands, and musicals. As Strait Music’s band and orchestra manager, Nathan likes to keep things positive, fun, and efficient in an effort to create a productive and pleasant atmosphere. In his spare time, Nathan enjoys cooking with friends, hunting for antiques, collecting vinyl records, and spending time with his pets Geppetto and Riko.

Ben George Band & Orchestra Sales

When asked what three words define himself, Ben chose the following: poised, unperturbed, and goofy. Raised on a farm on a little plot of land off the Pedernales River, Ben was surrounded by the folk guitar playing of his mother and step father. He later found an appreciation for the saxophone and jazz music. After being granted multiple merits in high school, Ben decided to attend Georgia State University's Jazz Performance Program. Upon leaving school, Ben moved back to Central Texas to continue to cultivate his sound and work at one of the best privately-owned music stores in the nation.

Emma Philips-Strain Band & Orchestra Sales

After a string of very quirky jobs, Emma now works in band and orchestra sales at Strait Music’s South location. Emma spends most of her free time rehearsing and performing with her two bands, The Haberdashers and Emma Kate, and snuggling her hedgehog Bruce Quillis.
